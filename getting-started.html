Fyenance: Offline Personal Finance Tracking App

Fyenance is a robust offline desktop application designed to help individuals manage and track their personal finances efficiently. Built with Electron, Fyenance offers a comprehensive suite of features that enable users to monitor their accounts, transactions, recurring payments, categories, and generate insightful financial reports. With a focus on security, usability, and functionality, Fyenance serves as an essential tool for personal financial planning and management.

Purpose

Fyenance aims to provide users with a secure and efficient platform to manage their personal finances offline. By consolidating various financial aspects into a single application, Fyenance helps users:

Monitor multiple financial accounts including bank accounts, credit cards, and cash.

Manage Transactions

Record income and expenses with detailed descriptions and categorization.

Automate Recurring Payments

Set up and manage recurring transactions such as bills and subscriptions.

Organize Categories

Create and manage categories to categorize financial transactions for better insights.

Generate Reports

Produce detailed financial reports and analytics to understand spending habits and trends.

Data Security

Ensure data privacy by storing all financial information locally without requiring internet access.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Fyenance.

Is Fyenance available for free?

Fyenance requires a valid license key for full access. You can purchase a license here

Can I use Fyenance offline?

Yes, Fyenance is designed to work entirely offline, ensuring your financial data remains private and secure on your device.

How do I update Fyenance?

Fyenance includes an automatic update feature. Navigate to Settings > Update to check for and install the latest updates manually if needed.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. Fyenance stores all your financial data locally on your device using a secure SQLite database. Ensure you regularly back up your data using the export feature.

How can I back up my data?

You can back up your data by exporting the database or exporting your data to CSV files. Go to Settings > Data Management to access these options.

Can I restore my data on a new device?

Yes, by exporting your data from your current device and importing it on the new device using the Import Database or Import CSV features.

What if I forget my license key?

If you have lost your license key, please contact our support team at support@metromagic.io

How do I delete all my data?

To delete all your data, navigate to Settings > Data Management and use the Delete Database option.

This action is irreversible and will permanently erase all your financial information.

Can I customize categories?

Yes, Fyenance allows you to create, edit, and delete custom categories to better fit your financial tracking needs.

Is there a mobile version?

Currently, Fyenance is available as a desktop application. Mobile support may be available in future releases.
