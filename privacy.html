Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13th, 2024

Introduction

Fyenance is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application and website.

Information We Collect

Desktop Application

Fyenance's desktop application operates locally on your computer. We collect:

  • License key information for activation purposes
  • Email address (only if provided for license activation)

Website Analytics

On our website (fyenanceapp.com), we use:

  • Google Analytics to understand visitor numbers and behavior
  • Microsoft Clarity to improve website usability

These analytics tools use cookies and similar technologies to collect:

  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Approximate location (city level only)

You can opt-out of analytics tracking through our cookie consent banner.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • License validation and management
  • Providing technical support
  • Improving our application and website
  • Understanding how visitors use our website
  • Sending important updates about the software

Data Storage and Security

Desktop Application: All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal financial information.

Website: Analytics data is processed by Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of website analytics tracking
  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data

Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Analytics (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity)
  • Cookie consent preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:
